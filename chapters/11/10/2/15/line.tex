It is obvious that the normal vector to the line is 
\begin{align}
\vec{n} =\myvec{2 \\ -9} -\vec{0} 
=\myvec{2 \\ -9}
\end{align}
Hence, the equation of the line is 
\begin{align}
	\myvec{2 & -9}\brak{\vec{x} - \myvec{2 \\ -9}}&= 0
	\\
	\implies 
	\myvec{2 & -9}\vec{x} &= 85
\end{align}
