		Following the approach in \probref{prob:12/11/3/9/plane},
		the desired equation is

\myvec{	1&-7}\vec{x} -5
+

	\\
	\implies 
	\myvec{	1 + 3k&-7+k}

	\myvec{	1 + 3k \\ -7+k}  = \alpha \myvec{1 \\ 0}

\end{align}
