\begin{align}
\myvec{\vec{B}-\vec{A} & \vec{C}-\vec{B}} 
=
		\myvec{6&-3\\-4&2} \xleftrightarrow{R_{2}\rightarrow R_{2}+\frac{2}{3}R_{1}}= \myvec{6&-3\\0&0}
\end{align}
which is a rank 1 matrix.  Hence, $\vec{A}, \vec{B}, \vec{C}$  are collinear.
